What I Do

Crafting experiences
room by room

01
Interior Visualization

Photorealistic 3D renders and mood boards that bring your vision to life before a single nail is hammered. See your future space in stunning detail.

02
Space Transformation

Full-room redesigns from concept to completion. I handle furniture layout, color palettes, lighting plans, and all the details that make a space sing.

03
Aesthetic Consulting

Not sure where to start? I'll guide you through your style — analyzing Pinterest boards, lifestyle needs, and budget to create your signature look.

04
Color Psychology

Colors affect how we feel. I curate palettes that match your emotional goals — calming bedrooms, energizing kitchens, inspiring home offices.

05
Lighting Design

From ambient glow to dramatic focal points, lighting transforms everything. I design layered lighting plans that work beautifully day and night.

06
Furniture Curation

Sourcing and styling furniture pieces that balance aesthetics and budget — creating compositions that feel intentional, curated, and uniquely yours.
